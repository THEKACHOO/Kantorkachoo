Kachoo Coordination Hub

Welcome to the Kachoo Coordination Office – the entry point for all manuscripts, products, news, and journals before they're published. This is where creativity begins its journey to becoming something tangible.

Address: Jenderal Sudirman Avenue, Lots 61–62, South Jakarta 12930, Indonesia

About This Office

Our Role

This office serves as the coordination and registration hub for all Kachoo publications. Every manuscript, news piece, and journal that will be published under the Kachoo brand starts its journey here. Once processed, materials are sent to our main production facility.

Building Design

The building features a brown color scheme with bright gray accents. Upon arrival, you'll notice a small front garden and a spacious parking area on the side of the building. The design balances professionalism with creative energy.

Specialized Coordination

Unlike our main production office, this location focuses solely on initial review, coordination, and registration. We handle the first steps of the publishing process, ensuring everything is organized before heading to production.

How It Works

Every piece of content goes through a streamlined process at our coordination hub before reaching production.

1

Submission

Authors and creators submit their manuscripts, articles, or journals to our office.

2

Initial Review

Our team conducts the first review and categorization of all materials.

3

Coordination

Materials are assigned to appropriate departments and scheduled for production.

4

Transfer

Organized materials are sent to our main production facility for publishing.

Office Facilities

Our coordination hub is designed to support every step of the initial publishing process. Here's what you'll find inside:

Main Lobby

A welcoming space with reception desk and waiting chairs for visitors and creators.

Public Relations

The first room where initial contacts and public interactions are managed.

Editorial Office

Coordinates manuscripts and books scheduled for publication on Kafkapages.

Kazine Division

Dedicated space for managing Kazine magazine publications and content.

Kaboom Press Room

Media and press coordination hub for Kaboom publications and announcements.

Dining Area

Large central space for lunch breaks and coffee breaks for all employees.

Restrooms

Separate facilities for male and female employees and visitors.

Back Garden

A small outdoor area for relaxation and smoking, with open airflow to dissipate smoke.

Ready for Production?

Once materials are coordinated here, they move to our main production facility for the final publishing process.
